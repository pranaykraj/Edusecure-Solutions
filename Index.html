About EduSecure Solutions

EduSecure Solutions is a brand-new startup focused on securing and optimizing Learning Management Systems (LMS) for schools, training institutes, and growing organizations. We’re starting small—with a security-first mindset, thoughtful processes, and a promise to be transparent at every step.

We work primarily with Moodle and open-source tools to deliver reliable setup, hardening, administration, and day-to-day support. As a new company, we trade “years of legacy” for fresh ideas, adaptability, and attentive service. Your success becomes our track record.

If you’re looking for a partner who will listen closely, iterate quickly, and align to your budget, we’d love to pilot a solution with you.
